About

The Berryman Pub in Ottawa opened in the summer of 2011. It is a sister pub of The Great Canadian Pub in Paris, France (www.TGCparis.com) which is widely regarded as the city's best pub. Expect The Berryman Pub to set the standard in food, service and sport's coverage for pub's in the Ottawa area. The Berryman Pub will raise the bar for all pubs in Ottawa which can only be a good thing. The brunch menu will make it a weekend favourite for all. Enjoy Ottawa!!
